Introduction to Algebra

Algebra is a branch of mathematics that uses letters and symbols to represent numbers and quantities in formulas and equations. It's a powerful tool that helps us solve real-world problems and understand patterns.

üéØ Why Learn Algebra?

In algebra, we use:

A system of equations is two or more equations with the same variables. The solution is the point where all equations are true at the same time.
